Lightning T.Mk.5

Pilot's Notes

1. Introduction

The English Electric Lightning T.Mk.5 is a two-seat, twin-engined supersonic trainer, having highly swept, mid-mounted main planes and a slab tailplane. The ailerons, rudder and tailplane are fully power operated and incorporate artificial feel for each control; hydraulically operated air brakes are trailing edge flaps are fitted. A braking parachute is housed in a compartment on the underside of the fuselage.

The power units are Avon Mk.210 axial-flow gas turbine engines, each developing approximately 11,200 lb static trust at sea level (14,400 lb approximately with maximum reheat). The engines are mounted in the fuselage, No. 2 to the rear of and above No. 1.
